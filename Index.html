About KICC

The Kenyatta International Convention Centre (KICC) is a state corporation under the Ministry of Tourism established by the Tourism Act, 2011. KICC is the pioneer and market leader in the Meetings, Incentives, Conferences and Exhibitions (MICE) industry in Kenya.

Our Mission

To position Kenya as a world-class destination for conventions, conferences and exhibitions through the provision of quality facilities and services.

Our Vision

To be the leading Meetings, Incentives, Conferences and Exhibitions (MICE) destination in Africa.

Our World-Class Facilities

Plenary Hall

Our flagship facility with a capacity of 4,000 delegates. Equipped with state-of-the-art interpretation booths, digital voting systems, and advanced lighting.

Meeting Rooms

Over 20 meeting rooms of varying sizes, from intimate boardrooms for 20 delegates to large conference halls seating 800.

Exhibition Space

10,000 square meters of flexible exhibition space with modern amenities for trade shows, product launches, and cultural exhibitions.

Helipad

Africa's first rooftop helipad offering convenient access for VIPs and aerial views of Nairobi.

Major Events at KICC

United Nations Conferences

KICC has hosted numerous UN conferences including UNEP Governing Council meetings and UN-HABITAT Assemblies.

WTO Ministerial Conference

Hosted the World Trade Organization's 10th Ministerial Conference in 2015 with delegates from 162 countries.

African Union Summits

Regular host of African Union meetings and summits, fostering continental cooperation.

KICC features a distinctive cylindrical tower inspired by traditional African huts, with a rotating restaurant offering panoramic city views.

National Monument

In 2015, KICC was gazetted as a National Monument, recognizing its architectural and historical importance to Kenya.
